Timeline

  • 1799 - Born on May 21st in Lyme Regis, Dorset.
  • 1811 - At 12 years old, Anning discovers her first Ichthyosaurus skull.
  • 1823 - Discovery of the first complete Plesiosaurus.
  • 1828 - Discovery of the first British Pterosaur specimen.
  • 1834 - Mary assists Louis Agassiz in his study of fossils.
  • 1847 - Anning dies from breast cancer at age 47.
